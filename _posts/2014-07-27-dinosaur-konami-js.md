"Finally a Konami Code Plugin I can use"

After long design and development process I am pleased to announce the first release of <a href='http://innesm4.github.io/dinosaur_konami.js'>Dinosaur Konami JS</a> version 1.0.

Dinosaur Konami JS is a a problem solver. 

Have you every wanted to put a dinosaur on your corporate website but couldn't get the idea past management? 

Have you ever thought "The only way I can take my sites UX to the next level is by having a dinosaur"?

If your answer is yes then finally a solution is now available for you.

Introducing <a href='http://innesm4.github.io/dinosaur_konami.js'>Dinosaur Konami JS</a>, the lightweight plugin that transforms any website into a dinosaur powered website.
